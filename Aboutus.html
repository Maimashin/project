Company Background

Contact One Mai-Maishin Nigeria Limited is a fully-fledged Nigerian company, incorporated in 2008 with its registered office at NO 30, Kwatarkwashi Road Road, Gusau Zamfara State. Contact One Mai-Maishin Nig. Ltd continues to operate under the basic principles of “Respect for the Individual” and “The Three Joys” — commonly expressed as The Joy of BuyingThe Joy of Selling and The Joy of Creating. “Respect for the Individual” reflects our desire to respect the unique character and ability of each individual person, trusting each other as equal partners to do our best in every situation. Based on this, “The Three Joys” expresses our belief and desire that each person working in, or coming into contact with our company, directly or through our products, should share a sense of joy through that experience.

About Us

HMN has since its incorporation and in line with its vision and philosophy been a proud partner of the government and the good people of Nigeria and Ogun State in particular. The Company in its over 40years of existence is proud to be a part of the industrial landscape changes of Ogun State and by extension Nigeria with all its wonderful and consistently reliable products such as the Ace and Dream model Motorcycles, FA and EG Generator series and other power products. The company is also actively involved in building the human capacity and potentials of its entire workforce, business partners and customers.

In addition, the company remains committed and is passionate about its corporate social responsibility. Towards this end, Honda has conducted several safety riding seminars in various parts of the country and other parts of Nigeria for both commercial and private motorcycle riders. In addition, HMN has provided regular support to the FRSC, the Nigerian Police and other governmental agencies in creating necessary and conducive environment for the positive wellbeing of its community.
